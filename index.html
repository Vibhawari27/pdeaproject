Tom And Jerry

Tom and Jerry is a classic animated television series created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera, first airing in 1940. It follows the comedic rivalry between Tom, a house cat, and Jerry, a clever mouse. The animation is renowned for its visual humor, minimal dialogue, and exceptional timing, making it one of the most beloved and enduring animated franchises in history.
